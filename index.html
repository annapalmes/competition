RULES

  • The teams must be between 8 and 10 members.
  • Each team chooses the way to participate; whether they want to dance together, in pairs or individually.
  • The teams will dance in the middle of the track in the marked diameter.
  • The winner will be chosen by the judges.
  • The winner will pass to the next round the loser will be eliminated directly.
  • In total, 8 groups will participate.

COURT

  • They will be professional dancers, most of them ex competitors.
  • They will be odd to avoid ties.
  • In the case that the judges decide that we have an tie, the battle will be make it again and again till the court decide a winner.
  • The judges decide the winner by pointing on the same time their favorite.
